# Commercial Licence

Commute Compute is available under a commercial licence for organisations that cannot comply with the AGPL v3's open-source requirements.

## When do I need a commercial licence?

You need a commercial licence if you want to:

- Include Commute Compute in a closed-source product
- Offer Commute Compute as a proprietary SaaS without sharing your source code
- Distribute modifications without open-sourcing them

## What does the commercial licence include?

- Right to use, modify, and distribute without AGPL requirements
- No obligation to disclose source code
- Terms tailored to your organisation's needs
